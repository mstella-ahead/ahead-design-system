Overview

How we cut p99 latency by 72% across 14 markets

A multi-region migration from monolithic infra to an edge-cached, Kubernetes-native delivery platform — designed, integrated, and shipped in eight months.

Step 01

Discovery and assessment

A two-week deep-dive across application, infra, and observability layers established the baseline and surfaced the real bottlenecks — most of which weren't where the team initially suspected.

Step 02

Edge-first architecture

We rebuilt the delivery layer around CDN-resident compute, with origin services treated as a fallback rather than the default path.

The team didn't just deliver — they re-shaped how we think about latency as a discipline, not an outcome.
